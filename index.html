    Instant messaging app for business

    Pumble is a real-time messaging app with a focus on speed and security. You can use Pumble on all your devices at the same time — your messages sync seamlessly across any number of your phones, or computers.

    Pumble is for everyone who wants fast and reliable messaging and calls. Reach your customers on a more personal level through Pumble instant messaging, a convenient instant messaging platform to send customers messages related to your brand all in the same thread, be they promotional, transactional, or conversational.

    With Pumble, you’ll be allowed to:

    Join and collaborate from anywhere,

    Discuss tasks easily with other team members via channels,

    Share files with your teammates,

    Collaborate across multiple devices (web, desktop, and mobile),

    Present and discuss ideas through video calls and screen sharing,

    Invite guests to join you in the conversations you choose.

    How does instant messaging app work?

    Get started with the most user friendly instant communication app for all kinds of teams.

    STEP 1

    Create a Pumble account

    To start communication with your team, the first thing you need to do is create an account.

    Sign up here

    STEP 2

    Create channels

    For easier communication in your workplace communication software, create channels related to projects like #alpha-project, #corporate-responsibility, #non-profit-projects or divide them per team - #marketing, #development, #design…

    STEP 3

    Set up your profile

    Set up your profile to give an example to others on how to set their profiles on Pumble. Insert your name and picture, position, and email address so others know how to contact you when you’re not available.

    STEP 4

    Invite your team

    Next, form teams. In Pumble, you can invite an unlimited number of people for FREE. Also, give admin roles so other people can help you manage other team members.

    STEP 5

    Share files with your clients

    To help you work with your team in Pumble, you can add files to your workspace. Files are searchable and shareable across Pumble, making it easier for everyone to find the information they need and work together.

    STEP 6

    Invite external collaborators

    In the Pumble BUSINESS plan, you can add up to five single-channel guests for every member of your existing team, free of charge. If you create channels for every project you have, use the single-channel guest feature to let in specialized outside individuals, like contractors, interns, and clients.
